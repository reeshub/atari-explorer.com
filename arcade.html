Video Games :: Arcade

It all started over 30 years ago, when Pong became the first commercially successful video game to make a profit.  It was simple in design, and highly playable in its execution.  Nobody had seen this type of entertainment anywhere before - you could actually control the objects on the screen, it was science fiction, surely?  This was 1972 wasn't it?

This were the infant days for the video game - and the video games culture would still be confined to the home for some time yet.  It wouldn't be until the mid-late 70's that video games invaded our homes in any reasonable number, but it was definitely the defining decade for the arcade gaming industry.
