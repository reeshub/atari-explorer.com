Updated site information for the Atari Museum and Atari Explorer...
 

21st December 2003 (Atari Explorer/Atari Museum)

It's been a busy month!  First we have the newly found Panther drawings and then we unearth some great new images of the Jaguar Duo console, thanks to Bruce Eng for those.  Also this month, we have software costings for Jaguar software and new shots of the Falcon 040 Microbox.  Over at the Atari Museum many new menu's are now live, and new updates will be available early next year.  Another new addition to AEX will be announced shortly.
 

3rd September 2003 (Atari Explorer)

Some small updates to the following sections:  History file updates (various), ST Book FDD proto casing added, ST section (various), XE Section (various), added the official Atari Developers Newsletter "RSC" December 1992 to the Falcon section.  Added a link to the official Space Invaders Anniversary website (home page).  More to come...
 

18th July 2003 (Atari Explorer)

After what seems a life time, AEX has been updated.  With a spotlight on ST and Atari 8-Bit gaming, new pictures throughout the site, an XE cartridge rarity guide, updates to the Jaguar and Lynx sections and some well needed history file updates... But its only half of what's to come to make this a real 2nd Edition, so we called it 1.5 instead!

February '03 - present  (Atari Museum)

The Atari Museum continues to be updated, with many new menus keeping with the unique Atari theme.  The real work is behind the scenes, with a new Server location dedicated to the site, as well as a second Archives server for what will become the largest single source for Atari information.  Keep a close eye on the site, its a continuous upgrade which we hope to have complete by the end of this year.

5th January 2003 (Atari Explorer)

Atari Explorer (.com) finally goes online.
